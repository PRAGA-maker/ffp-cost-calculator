Post-2029 Sustainability

All evidence-based practices required by BH-CONNECT have permanent Medi-Cal billing authority under State Plan Amendments 24-0042 (ACT/FACT), 24-0051 (IPS, Peer Support), and 24-0052 (Enhanced CHW).

If BH-CONNECT is not renewed after December 2029, the community programs you build continue billing regular Medi-Cal. Only the institutional matching (FFP on IMD stays) would cease.

Post-2029 stranded cost: approximately $0.

Context

What is BH-CONNECT? California's Section 1115 demonstration (approved by CMS Dec 2024) allows counties to claim 50% federal Medicaid matching on eligible services in qualifying IMDs through Dec 2029. Three counties approved: Sacramento, San Diego, Santa Clara. 15 more planning to opt in.

What are qualifying facilities? Mental Health Rehabilitation Centers (MHRCs), Psychiatric Health Facilities (PHFs), and freestanding Acute Psychiatric Hospitals (APHs) on the DHCS IMD list. SNF/STP facilities, QRTPs, and general hospital psych units do not qualify.

National evidence: 15 states with IMD waivers show 14% fewer psychiatric inpatient admissions, 26% fewer ED visits, 11% fewer incarcerations, 41% lower psychiatric costs, with no overall cost increase (AJMC, 2025, 3.2M beneficiaries).
